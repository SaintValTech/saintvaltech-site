About SaintValTech

Hi, I’m Emeka Valentine Ogbu, founder of SaintValTech. I focus on Security Operations (SOC), detection engineering, incident response, and vulnerability management.

My work emphasizes governed security operations — ensuring security activities such as scanning, detection, and response are expected, auditable, and measurable rather than reactive.

I build and document enterprise-style security labs that mirror real-world SOC workflows using SIEM, IDS, EDR, and identity systems.
